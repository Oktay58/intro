About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!
My name is Oktay Tecer, and I'm a 17-year-old student pursuing a career in software development at Grafisch Lyceum Rotterdam. I'm passionate about technology and the limitless possibilities it offers in shaping our future.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have a deep-rooted love for gaming. Whether I'm exploring vast virtual worlds, solving intricate puzzles, or competing in multiplayer battles, video games have always been a source of inspiration and entertainment for me. I see them not only as a form of recreation but also as an art form that combines storytelling, design, and technology.

When I'm not immersed in the digital realm, you can often find me on the basketball court. Playing basketball is not just a physical activity for me, it's a way to challenge myself, stay active, and foster teamwork. The synergy between teamwork on the court and collaborative coding in software development is a connection I find intriguing.

Throughout this portfolio, I look forward to sharing my journey as a software developer, showcasing the projects I've worked on, and highlighting how my love for gaming and basketball has influenced my approach to problem-solving and innovation. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work and insights.
